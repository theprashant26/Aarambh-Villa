Noida Extension, now known as Greater Noida West, is a prime area known for its modern infrastructure and proximity to significant landmarks and facilities:

Prime Location

Welcome to Aarambh Valley Villas, an exceptional neighborhood offering the best of urban living with the tranquility of a suburban retreat. Nestled in the heart of the city, this location ensures easy access to top-rated schools like Sanskar Public School Greater Noida West and Little Scholars Academy, ensuring quality education close to home.

Connectivity & Infrastructure

Excellent connectivity with a good public transport system, including buses and metro.Well-organized roads with clear signage, enhancing ease of travel and safety.

Well-lit streets, regular police patrols, and high cleanliness standards make it a preferred living area.

What's New

New commercial projects, parking structures, and a proposed flyover at Mandi Marg to ease traffic congestion.

Direct route to the Hindon River and close proximity to the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway.
